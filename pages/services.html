Screen Printing

Screen printing is one of the most common methods of printing designs on objects. If you have any graphic tees in your closet, chances are they were screen printed at a shop just like ours! This is the method we'll use if you want the same graphic printed on hundreds or even thousands of shirts. Screen printed apparel will require a setup fee of $20 for each color in your graphic.

DTF (Direct-To-Film) Transfers

DTF transfers don't require any setup fees and is the better choice for smaller quantity orders or if your graphic contains a large amount of colors. These prints are applied with heat and may look more familiar to people who have seen t-shirts being printed at festivals and fairs. If your order includes names of individuals, a range of numbers (like team jerseys), or any gradients, DTF transfer is the way to go!

Special Effects

Add some fun to your prints with special effects like puff, metallic, or glow-in-the-dark. While these effects will increase the cost of each unit, they come with the benefit of having more eyes drawn on your brand, logo, or business name.
