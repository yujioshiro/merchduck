What is the order process?

  1. Order here.
  2. We’ll email you a final quote and mockup of prints.
  3. Once order is approved, we will print a single unit for you to preview prior to printing entire order.
  4. We’ll notify you when the order is complete.
  5. Pickup, delivery, and shipping available.

Where do I pick up my order?

We do not have a storefront as we run the business out of a garage located in Lake Forest, CA. Once your order is ready, we’ll text you the address and you can come pick it up whenever is convenient for you.

Do you deliver or ship?

Yes, we deliver within Southern California. Please make sure you include your address so we can give you an accurate price. Shipping prices will be calculated and listed in your quote.

Which method of printing is best for me?

It will depend on your graphic, needs, budget. Check out the differences here. If you’re still unsure, message us with some information about your order and we can determine the right method for you.
